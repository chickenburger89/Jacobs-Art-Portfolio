About

MedExplorer is a healthcare companion app designed to help educate people on healthcare and get the most out of their plans. Kameron, Laura, and myself wanted to address some of the pressing concerns regarding healthcare in the United States by creating an app that contains lots of easily accessible information. We undertook a lot of user research including interviews, surveys, and usability testing to determine the best way to design our app and what features to include.
