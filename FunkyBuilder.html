About

Funky Builder is a designed activity that is meant to be quick, fun, and invite creativity. I did a lot of user research including interviews, a survey, and usability testing to help inform my final design. I learned a lot about the human-centered design process during this assignment; I used the data gathered to help me iterate and build upon my ideas which resulted in the final experience. Doing this helped me become intimately familiar with the design process.
